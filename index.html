About

I'm a product manager currently living in Norfolk, Va. I moved here in August 2017 from Boston while my girlfriend attends medical school. I was born and raised on Cape Cod and am a proud alum of Boston College.

Previously I have worked as a Product Manager at sports software company Hudl, was the first non-technical hire at Ubersense and in advanced scouting for the New York Yankees.
